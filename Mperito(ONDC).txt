As a certified Seller-side Technology Service Provider (TSP) for ONDC, Mperito eliminates the technical complexity of joining India's most exciting e-commerce revolution.

Our comprehensive solution ensures your business is fully integrated across all aspects of the ONDC ecosystem - from catalogue management to order processing, logistics coordination to payment reconciliation.

What is ONDC and Why it Matters?
Unified Digital Commerce
ONDC (Open Network for Digital Commerce) is India's revolutionary network connecting sellers directly to multiple buyer apps like Paytm, Meesho, and Magicpin through a single integration.

Massive Market Access
Gain instant visibility to millions of customers across India without needing to list separately on each marketplace or build your own e-commerce platform.

Lower Customer Acquisition Costs
Escape high commission structures of traditional marketplaces and connect directly with customers through an open, interoperable network built for seller success.

Your ONDC Onboarding Journey
Leverage Mperito as TSP to make it Simple, Fast, Effective
Share Basic Business Details
Provide your business name, GSTIN, PAN, bank account information, and pickup address. We handle the paperwork and verification process required by ONDC.

Digitise Your Catalogue
Send us your product information, including pricing, images, and descriptions. Our team formats everything according to ONDC's strict standards and taxonomy requirements.

Complete ONDC Registration
We register your business on the ONDC network and secure your unique seller ID. This officially makes you part of India's open commerce revolution.

From Setup to Sales: The Final Steps
Technical Setup
We configure inventory management, connect to ONDC-approved logistics providers, and integrate secure payment gateways - all without requiring technical work from your side.

Testing & Launch
Before going live, we conduct thorough testing with internal orders to verify all systems are working perfectly. Once verified, your products appear across all ONDC buyer apps.

Growth & Optimisation
